Our story

Where craft
meets precision.

Founded in 2019 by Marius Boariu, Tudor Saloon was built on a single principle: every man deserves an exceptional barbershop experience. Not just a haircut — a ritual. A moment of care, skill, and attention to detail.

Located in the heart of Iași at Bulevardul Chimiei nr. 7, River Tower's, we've served thousands of clients with the same quiet commitment we started with.

Marius Boariu

Master Barber & Founder

Marius opened Tudor Saloon in 2019 with a clear vision — to raise the standard of barbering in Iași. Trained in classical technique and refined through years of practice, he brings the same focus to every client that he brings to competition.

His work has been recognised at national barber competitions, but the measure he cares most about is simpler: clients who leave looking and feeling their best.
