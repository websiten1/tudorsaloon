Our Story

WHERE CRAFT
MEETS STYLE.

Founded in 2019 by Marius Boariu, Tudor Saloon was built on a simple belief: every man deserves a great haircut and the confidence that comes with it. We're not just a barbershop — we're a space where tradition and modern grooming come together.

Located at Bulevardul Chimiei nr. 7, River Tower's, Iași, our doors are open six days a week. Walk in or book ahead — we're ready for you.
